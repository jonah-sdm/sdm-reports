Executive Summary

This position takes a long-term directional view on BTC through a long-dated call option expiring 26 Dec 2026 (300 days out). Rather than purchasing the asset outright, the portfolio acquires 10 call option contract(s) at a $95,000.00 strike price for $12,000.00 per unit, committing $120,000.00 in total capital. The maximum risk is limited to the $120,000.00 premium paid. The position breaks even at $107,000.00, requiring a 12.6% move from current levels.
